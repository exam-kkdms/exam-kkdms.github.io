KAMAL KUMARI DEVI MODEL SCHOOL (H.S)

  1. Maintain punctuality. Be present at the assigned exam room on time.
  2. Unauthorized leaves are not approved. Any issues arising due to such leaves must be clarified by the respective teacher only.
  3. Mobile phones are not allowed in the examination hall.
  4. Ensure every student's admit card is signed.
  5. Before signing the students’ answer sheets as an invigilator, check the details filled in by the students and verify them with the admit card.
  6. Count the total number of students and match it with the number of answer sheets.
  7. Arrange the answer sheets in class-wise chronological order.
  8. In the end note, please mention the class, section, subject, count of answer sheets, and roll numbers of absentees (if any).
  9. Answer sheet verification and attendance should be completed as early as possible.
  10. For any unfair means (UFM), cancel the paper and issue a new answer sheet to the student. Attach the necessary documents with the UFM answer sheet. If no proper document is available, describe the UFM on the answer sheet and take the student’s signature. If the student is non-cooperative, inform the examination management member.
  11. Do not leave the exam room without informing the authorized personnel, and hand over your duties to another invigilator.
  12. No student is allowed to take any exam materials—except the question paper—outside the examination room under any circumstances.
  13. It is the subject teacher's responsibility to give the answer sheets to the rechecker, collect them back, and submit them to the exam department within the given time.
  14. Recheckers should not sign the answer sheets; however, they must sign the recheck register in the exam department.
  15. Recheckers must prepare a report on the rechecked copy and submit it along with the rechecked answer sheet to the exam department.
  16. Subject teachers must record the students’ marks, upload them to the website immediately after evaluation, and update them post-recheck (if applicable).
  1. Students can be allowed up to 15 minutes of extra time if the invigilator feels it is necessary.
  2. Subject teachers may visit only once to clarify doubts. Invigilators must inform students that any incorrect question, if attempted, will still carry the allotted marks.
  3. Multiple students should not be allowed to go to the washroom at once. This rule is not applicable up to Class II.
  4. The 1st half exam floppy must be collected by the invigilator. The 2nd half exam floppy will be distributed by a member of the examination department.
